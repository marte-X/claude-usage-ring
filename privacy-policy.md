# Claude Usage Ring — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Claude Usage Ring does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension communicates exclusively with claude.ai using your existing 
browser session to retrieve your usage statistics (a percentage value and 
a reset timestamp). This data is stored locally on your device using 
chrome.storage.local and is never sent to any external server, database, 
or third party.

The extension does not access or collect:
- Your name, email address, or account details
- Your conversation content or messages
- Your authentication credentials
- Any personally identifiable information
